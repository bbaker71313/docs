Getting your account set up correctly takes about three minutes and makes every scan result accurate for your specific business. This page walks you through creating an account, verifying your email, and configuring your profit settings — the numbers that drive every HOT, LIST, and SKIP decision you receive.

## Creating your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the app">
    Open **[scanforprofit.com/app.html](https://scanforprofit.com/app.html)** in any browser on your phone or computer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Tap **Sign Up**. Enter your email address and choose a password. Tap **Create Account**.

    <Note>
      The free **Scout** plan activates immediately — no credit card required. Scout gives you **25 AI scans per month** and up to **10 inventory items**. You can upgrade to a paid plan at any time from the Settings screen.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    ScanForProfit sends a verification email to the address you entered. Open the email and click the verification link to activate your account. You must complete this step before you can log in.

    <Warning>
      Check your **spam or junk folder** if the email doesn't arrive within a few minutes. The verification link expires after a period of time — if yours has expired, log in to the app and request a new verification email.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Logging in

Once your email is verified, return to **[scanforprofit.com/app.html](https://scanforprofit.com/app.html)**, enter your email and password, and tap **Log In**.

<Note>
  ScanForProfit uses email and password sign-in only. There are no magic links or third-party login options.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Add the app to your phone's home screen for one-tap access from the field. On iPhone, tap the share icon in Safari and choose **Add to Home Screen**. On Android, tap the menu in Chrome and choose **Add to Home Screen**.
</Tip>

## The Settings screen

Your Settings screen is where you tell ScanForProfit your actual business numbers. Every profit estimate and buy decision is calculated using these values, so the closer they are to your real costs and fee rates, the more accurate your results.

To open Settings, tap the **gear icon** at the top of the **Profit Scanner** tab.

### eBay fee settings

| Setting | What it controls | Default value |
|---|---|---|
| **eBay Fee %** | The final value fee rate eBay charges you on each sale | 13% |
| **Shipping Cost** | Your average cost to ship a sold item | What you typically pay at the post office or UPS |
| **Packaging Cost** | Average cost of boxes, poly mailers, bubble wrap, etc. | $1.25 |

<Tip>
  eBay fee rates range from 8% to 18% depending on your seller category and account standing. Sellers in categories like media (books, DVDs) often pay lower rates; electronics and clothing sellers often pay higher rates. Set your rate accurately — a 5% difference can flip a SKIP into a LIST.
</Tip>

### Profit targets

| Setting | What it controls | Default value |
|---|---|---|
| **Min Profit ($)** | Minimum net profit required for a LIST or HOT decision | $15 |
| **Target ROI (%)** | Target return on investment to qualify as HOT | 200% |
| **Max Days to Sell** | How long you're willing to hold an item before it's stale | 60 days |

Items that meet your min profit target come back as LIST. Items that also hit your target ROI come back as HOT. Items that fall below your min profit come back as SKIP.

### Resetting settings to defaults

If you want to start fresh, scroll to the bottom of the Settings screen and tap **Reset to Defaults**. This restores all profit targets and fee settings to the app's recommended starting values. Your inventory and scan history are not affected.

<Warning>
  Resetting to defaults overwrites your custom fee rates and profit targets. Jot down your current settings before you reset if you want to restore them later.
</Warning>

## Your account tier

Your current plan is shown at the top of the Settings screen under **Account**. ScanForProfit offers four tiers:

| Plan | Price | Scans/month | Inventory items |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Scout** | Free | 25 | 10 |
| **Hustle** | $19/mo | Unlimited | 500 |
| **Stack** | $49/mo | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| **Empire** | $199/mo | Unlimited | Unlimited (up to 10 seats) |

<Note>
  When you reach your monthly scan limit on the Scout plan, scans are paused until the next billing cycle or until you upgrade. Your inventory and all past scan history are preserved either way.
</Note>

Tap **Upgrade Plan** in the Settings screen to see full plan details and switch tiers. See the [Plans & Pricing](/plans/overview) page for a complete feature comparison.

## Logging out

To log out, scroll to the bottom of the Settings screen and tap **Log Out**. Your inventory and settings are saved to your account — everything will be exactly where you left it when you log back in.

<Tip>
  ScanForProfit stores your inventory data in your account so it's available across devices. You can log in from your phone at the thrift store and from your computer at home and see the same inventory.
</Tip>
