ScanForProfit is a web app built for eBay resellers who source inventory at thrift stores, estate sales, garage sales, and flea markets. Point your phone's camera at any item — or an entire shelf — and get an instant **HOT**, **LIST**, or **SKIP** decision with real profit math calculated after your exact eBay fees. No manual research, no spreadsheets, no mental math while someone else is eyeing the same item.

## The problem it solves

Every profitable sourcing decision happens at the shelf, but until now resellers had no fast, reliable way to make those decisions in the moment. Manual eBay sold-listing searches take 2–5 minutes per item. At a thrift store with 200 items, that math doesn't work. And even when you do the research, you still have to calculate fees by hand, re-enter data into a spreadsheet, and switch between four different apps to write a listing and track what you made.

ScanForProfit replaces that entire stack — the eBay Seller Hub tab, the Google Sheet, the notes app, and the random scanner — with one integrated tool that starts from a photo and ends with a real P&L number.

## How it's different

Other sourcing tools solve one piece of the puzzle. ScanForProfit solves the whole workflow.

| | ScanForProfit | Typical scanner app | Manual eBay search |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Whole-shelf scan** | ✅ One photo, every item ranked | ❌ One item at a time | ❌ |
| **Profit after fees** | ✅ Your exact eBay rate | ⚠️ Fixed rate or none | ❌ You do the math |
| **Inventory tracking** | ✅ Built in | ❌ Separate tool needed | ❌ |
| **Listing generator** | ✅ Built in | ❌ | ❌ |
| **P&L dashboard** | ✅ Built in | ❌ | ❌ |
| **No API key needed** | ✅ | Varies | ✅ |

**Shelf scan is unique.** No other consumer reseller tool lets you photograph an entire shelf and get every item ranked by profit potential. It's how serious resellers walk an estate sale in 3 minutes instead of 90.

**Configurable fee math sets the profit numbers apart.** Your eBay fee rate depends on your seller category and tier — anywhere from 8% to 18%. ScanForProfit lets you set your exact rate so every profit estimate is accurate for *your* business, not a generic average.

**One integrated workflow means nothing falls through the cracks.** A scan leads directly to inventory, which leads directly to a listing, which feeds directly into your P&L. No re-entering data. No copy-pasting between apps.

## Who it's for

ScanForProfit is built for solo resellers and small operations (1–3 people) who:

- Source from thrift stores, estate sales, garage sales, or flea markets
- Sell primarily on eBay (Hustle tier and above also support Mercari and Poshmark)
- Are doing 20 to 200+ items per month
- Are currently spending more time researching and tracking than actually sourcing

If you're an Amazon FBA seller sourcing new products with barcodes, tools like Jungle Scout are a better fit for your workflow. ScanForProfit is optimized for the mixed-category, no-barcode world of secondhand reselling.
