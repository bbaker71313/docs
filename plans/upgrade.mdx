Upgrading your plan takes about two minutes and happens entirely inside the app. There's no website to visit and no form to fill out — tap through to checkout, enter your payment details, and your new limits activate the moment payment processes.

## Upgrade Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Profit Hub">
    Tap the **Stats** tab at the bottom of the screen, then tap **Subscription** in the sub-navigation at the top.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your plan">
    You'll see the plan cards for Hustle, Stack, and Empire with feature highlights. Tap the plan you want to move to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select monthly or annual billing">
    Toggle between **Monthly** and **Annual** billing. Annual billing saves you over 20% — see the breakdown in the next section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    Tap **Upgrade**. You'll be taken to a secure checkout screen. Enter your payment details and confirm. Your new plan activates immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Monthly vs. Annual Pricing

Annual billing locks in a lower monthly rate and charges you once per year.

| Plan | Monthly | Annual (billed yearly) | Annual savings |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Hustle** | $19/mo | $15/mo ($180/yr) | Save $48/yr |
| **Stack** | $49/mo | $39/mo ($468/yr) | Save $120/yr |
| **Empire** | $199/mo | $159/mo ($1,908/yr) | Save $480/yr |

If you're already sure you'll use ScanForProfit for the long haul, annual is the better deal. If you want to try a paid plan for a month or two first, start monthly — you can switch to annual at any time.

## What Happens Immediately After Upgrading

Your new plan takes effect the moment your payment is confirmed:

- **Scan limits reset** — your monthly scan count resets to unlimited (Hustle, Stack, Empire) immediately
- **Inventory cap lifts** — you can add items beyond the previous limit right away
- **New features unlock** — P&L dashboard, photo enhancement, Profit Compass, bulk listing, and other plan-specific features become available instantly
- **No app restart required** — the app detects your new tier automatically

## Managing Your Subscription

### Cancel your plan

You can cancel at any time. Go to **Stats → Subscription → Manage Subscription**. This opens the billing portal where you can cancel, change your billing cycle, or update your payment method.

<Note>
  When you cancel, your paid access continues through the end of your current billing period. You won't be charged again, and your account moves to Scout (free) when the period ends. Your inventory data is never deleted.
</Note>

### Downgrade to a lower plan

You can downgrade to a lower paid plan or to the free Scout tier from the same billing portal. Select the plan you want, confirm the change, and it takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

<Warning>
  If you downgrade and your current inventory count exceeds the limit on the lower plan — for example, moving from Stack (unlimited) to Hustle (500 items) with 600 items in your inventory — you won't lose any items, but you won't be able to add new ones until you're back under the limit. Archive or delete older sold items to make room.
</Warning>

### Update payment method

From the billing portal (**Stats → Subscription → Manage Subscription**), you can update your credit or debit card at any time. Changes apply to your next billing cycle.
