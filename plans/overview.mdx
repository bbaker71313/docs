ScanForProfit is free to use from day one — no credit card, no trial period, no expiration. The free Scout plan gives you everything you need to get started: AI scans, profit calculations, a listing generator, and CSV export. Paid plans unlock higher scan and inventory limits, deeper analytics, and team features as your reselling business grows.

<Note>
  Scout is free forever. You never need a credit card to use it.
</Note>

## Which Plan Is Right for You?

<Accordion title="I flip on weekends or as a side hustle">
  Start with **Scout** (free). You get 25 AI scans per month — enough for a regular sourcing trip or two — plus the profit calculator, basic listing generator, and CSV export to eBay.

  Once you're running out of scans before the month ends, or you've got more than 10 items to track, step up to **Hustle**. At $19/month, one extra good flip more than covers the subscription.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I resell full-time or source multiple times a week">
  **Stack** is built for you. Unlimited scans and inventory mean the app never slows you down. Profit Compass watches your inventory and tells you what categories to focus on, what to reprice, and what to hunt for this week. Stale alerts catch listings that have been sitting too long before they become dead stock.

  If you're currently on Hustle and hitting 500 items or wishing the app could surface more strategic insights, Stack is the right move.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I run a team or work with a partner">
  **Empire** gives you up to 10 team seats with shared inventory, so your whole team sees the same stock. The manager dashboard shows you performance across the operation — who's sourcing, what's selling, where the profit is coming from. API access lets you connect ScanForProfit to your own tools if you've outgrown the built-in workflow.
</Accordion>
