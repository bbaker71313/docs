Photograph any item — or an entire shelf — and get an instant HOT, LIST, or SKIP decision with real profit math after fees, all in under 8 seconds. No spreadsheets. No manual eBay research. No guessing.

## How it works

Three steps are all it takes to go from standing in a thrift store to knowing exactly what to buy.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Scan the item or shelf">
    Open [ScanForProfit](https://scanforprofit.com/app.html) in your browser, tap **Profit Scanner**, and take a photo of any item — or a wide shot of a whole shelf. You can also type the item name if you prefer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the decision">
    In under 8 seconds you get a **HOT**, **LIST**, or **SKIP** verdict along with estimated sell price, your profit after eBay fees, and comparable sold listings you can tap to verify.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to inventory and list it">
    Tap **Add to Inventory** for anything you're buying. When you're ready to sell, open the item in Inventory and use the **Listing Generator** to create a complete eBay listing in seconds. Track your P&L in the Profit Hub.
  </Step>
</Steps>
